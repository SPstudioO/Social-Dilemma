## Research & Reference
#### * The Social Dilemma
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/94651916/142584150-5715a110-c263-4e70-b550-7973285abaac.jpeg)

Social Dilemma, a movie available on Nexflix, is a documentary film that contains warning messages by Silicon Valley experts who have developed social media platforms these days living in a modern society suffering form Social media addiction and Fake news. Tech companies such as Google, Facebook, Instagram are required to identify users and classify their propensity througth the internet. As it repeats, so people are becoming to see content that is biased toward only their interests. Even compaines that use it to take advantage of fake advertising and fake news are becoming more extreme, even though, it's mainly about tech companies that have created social media platforms are not being held accountable for this phenomenon.
